Immersive volumetric rendering of Medical volumes

RealVol features a virtual reality (VR) based immersive walkthrough inside a CT or MRI volume. We use our own in-house renderer that optimises dual rendering for real-time visualization in VR. State of the art graphics hardware and VR headsets are used to provide un-hindererd diagnostics. We use advanced volumetric algorithm to directly convert 2D scan images to 3D volumes for interactive visualisations.
